Troubleshooting
Rates won't load ▼
Check your internet connection and try refreshing the page. Tap the amber rate badge at the top of the app to manually refresh.
A subscriber says their payment didn't go through ▼
Bitcoin payments can take a few minutes to confirm on the blockchain. Ask the subscriber to check their wallet app for a pending transaction. Do not mark a payment as received until it confirms.
I can't log in ▼
Request a new magic link by entering your email on the login screen. Check your spam folder if it doesn't arrive within a minute. Magic links expire after 15 minutes.
How do I issue a refund? ▼
Bitcoin payments cannot be reversed once confirmed on the blockchain. To refund a subscriber, you must send Bitcoin back to their wallet address manually. Make sure your refund policy is communicated before they subscribe.
